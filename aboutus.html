About Us

We, the Student Council of Vidyalankar Institute of Technology are a dynamic group of student leaders, committed to enhancing the academic as well as extracurricular experience of the students.

Our vision is to foster a supportive community, organize engaging events that are aligned with industrial and societal trends, events and workshops that are in line with students' interests and needs, provide them with a platform to showcase their talent and aid holistic development.

Together we strive to shape an inclusive college environment that is diverse and vibrant!

Where every student can explore something they relish and rest assured that their talent finds recognition!
